Having established the architectural foundations of LLMs in Part 1, we now turn our attention to the critical component that determines model performance: the data.

Part 2 focuses on the end-to-end data engineering pipeline. We will start by implementing scalable ingestion and ETL processes for various data formats. From there, we'll cover essential curation techniques, including deduplication and toxicity filtering, and explore the use of synthetic data for dataset enrichment. We will then analyze the mechanics of tokenization and the impact of vocabulary expansion. Finally, we'll examine how to structure datasets into prompt-response pairs to prepare the model for instruction tuning. This section provides the necessary framework to transform raw information into a curated training set.
